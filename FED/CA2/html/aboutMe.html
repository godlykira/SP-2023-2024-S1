So, who am I?

Hello everyone! My name is Min Thit Khine, and I am currently a DCITP student at Singapore Polytechnic.

One of my main interests is sustainable living and developing innovative solutions for reducing the negative effects of climate change. I'm convinced that small changes can have a big difference, so I am dedicated to utilizing eco-friendly practices into my daily life. From recycling and reducing waste to embracing renewable energy sources, I strive to live a low-carbon lifestyle.
