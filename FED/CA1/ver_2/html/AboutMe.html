Min Thit Khine


Hello everyone! My name is Min Thit Khine, and I am currently a DCITP student at Singapore Polytechnic. Today, I'd like to share with you my deep passion for environmental conservation, as well as how it aligns with my interests and personality.



One of my main interests is sustainable living and developing innovative solutions for reducing the negative effects of climate change. I'm convinced that small changes can have a big difference, so I am dedicated to utilizing eco-friendly practices into my daily life. From recycling and reducing waste to embracing renewable energy sources, I strive to live a low-carbon lifestyle.



Furthermore, I enjoy meeting people from various backgrounds and discussing ways to protect our planet. I am always eager to inspire and educate others about the environment, whether by organizing workshops, participating in green initiatives, or simply having conversations.

This mission to save the planet is not just a personal endeavor for me; it is a responsibility that we all share. I am convinced that collective action can have a positive impact on our environment. We can collectively work toward a more sustainable future by encouraging and empowering individuals aged 16 and up.

We can start by achieving these three long-term objectives.
